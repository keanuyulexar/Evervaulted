How Evervaulted Works

We’ve made it easy to secure your digital legacy in just five simple steps:

  1. Step 1: Create Your Secure Account
    Sign up and verify your identity. Your vault is encrypted from the start.

  2. Step 2: Upload Your Digital Will & Documents
    Store legal documents, identity info, financial assets, and anything else that matters.

  3. Step 3: Assign Access & Conditions
    Choose who gets access to your vault (family, lawyer, executor) — and when.

  4. Step 4: Add Legacy Messages
    Record video messages, write letters, and leave final words that will only be shared when the time is right.

  5. Step 5: Lock It & Relax
    Your vault is protected 24/7. Update your info anytime. We handle the rest securely.
